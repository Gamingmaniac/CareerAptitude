Guidelines for the Career Aptitude Test

  • Welcome to the Career Aptitude Test! Before you begin, please review the following important guidelines to ensure a smooth and meaningful experience:
  • 1. Purpose of the Test:
    • The questions are thoughtfully designed to assess your interests and preferences.
    • Your responses will help identify the field best suited to you: Arts, Science, or Commerce.
  • 2. Structure of the Test:
  • The test consists of 15 multiple choice questions.
  • Each question carries 1 point, contributing to your overall result.
  • 3. Result Presentation:
    • Upon completing the test, your results will be displayed in a graph format.
    • The graph will clearly indicate the field where your interests align, with the highest column representing your strongest preference.
    • Our AI-powered recommendation system will provide tailored insights based on your responses.
    • Take your time, answer honestly, and let this test guide you toward a career path that matches your unique interests and strengths.
    • So, “let's find your calling and shape your destiny, with AI!”
    • Best of luck!!!
