Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our services.

General Questions

What is Career Aptitude?

Career Aptitude is a platform designed to help individuals of 9th or 10th grade discover their ideal career paths based on their skills, interests, and knowledge.

Who can benefit from Career Aptitude?

9th or 10th graders looking to explore career options.

How does the assessment work?

Our assessment consists of a series of questions designed to evaluate your skills, interests, and values. Based on your responses, we provide personalized career recommendations.

Account and Registration

Do I need to create an account to use the services?

Yes, creating an account allows you to save your progress, access personalized recommendations, and receive updates.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and following the instructions.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account by going to your profile settings and selecting the option to delete your account.

Quiz and Assessments

How long does the quiz take?

The quiz typically takes about 25-30 minutes to complete, depending on your pace.

Is the quiz free?

Yes, our career assessment is free.

How accurate are the results?

While our assessments are designed to provide valuable insights, they are based on self-reported data. We recommend using the results as a guide rather than a definitive answer.

Support and Contact

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the Contact page or by emailing support@careeraptitude.com.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical issues, please contact our support team with details about the problem, and we will assist you as soon as possible.

Privacy and Security

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

Do you share my data with third parties?

No, we do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.
